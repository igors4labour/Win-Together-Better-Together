About Customer Success

Here's a quick rundown of what the Customer Success Team does and how we fit into everything else going on around here. It tells you what we do, who we work closely with, and how we help keep things running smoothly.

And if you're curious about anything else, feel free to pop over and ask.

Our Team Values in Action

‚ñº

Ignite Growth

  • We continuously learn from client feedback and use those insights to improve how we support their success
  • We share what works (and what doesn't) across client accounts so everyone benefits from our collective experience
  • We challenge ourselves to find better ways to drive client value, not just maintain the status quo

Empower Through Innovation

  • We take time to understand what clients actually need before proposing solutions or escalations
  • We look for practical approaches that work for each client's unique situation and challenges
  • When a support strategy isn't working, we're willing to change course and try different approaches

Always Go Beyond

  • We put ourselves in our clients' shoes when handling issues and understanding their business pressures
  • We respond quickly to client concerns and system issues, knowing that delays impact their operations
  • We focus our energy on activities that genuinely improve client satisfaction and system adoption

Trust Starts With Us

  • When we see potential issues (adoption challenges, system problems, churn risks), we flag them early rather than waiting
  • We keep clients informed about what's happening with their issues and requests, even when progress is slow
  • When we commit to follow-ups or resolutions, we do everything we can to follow through
  • We own our mistakes in client communication or issue handling and focus on fixing them rather than making excuses

Win Together

  • We value input from clients and internal teams - Customer Success brings relationship expertise, technical teams bring product knowledge
  • We work closely with Support, Product, and Engineering to ensure clients get the best possible outcomes
  • We celebrate client successes when they achieve great outcomes with the system
  • We approach every client interaction with respect and professionalism, even when conversations are difficult
