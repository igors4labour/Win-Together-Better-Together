About HR

Here's a quick rundown of what the HR Team does and how we fit into everything else going on around here. It tells you what we do, who we work closely with, and how we help keep things running smoothly.

And if you're curious about anything else, feel free to pop over and ask.

Our Team Values in Action

‚ñº

Ignite Growth

  • We continuously learn from employee feedback and use those insights to improve our policies and programmes
  • We share what works (and what doesn't) across teams so everyone can benefit from our learnings
  • We challenge ourselves to find better ways to support our people, not just stick with "how it's always been done"

Empower Through Innovation

  • We take time to understand what employees actually need before rolling out new policies or systems
  • We look for practical solutions that work for everyone, not just what sounds good on paper
  • When something isn't working, we're willing to change course and try a different approach

Always Go Beyond

  • We put ourselves in employees' shoes when handling sensitive situations or policy decisions
  • We respond quickly to questions and concerns, knowing that waiting causes unnecessary stress
  • We focus our energy on things that genuinely improve people's work lives

Trust Starts With Us

  • When we see potential issues (hiring delays, policy changes, system problems), we flag them early rather than waiting
  • We keep people informed about what's happening and why, even when the news isn't great
  • When we commit to a timeline or deliverable, we do everything we can to follow through
  • We own our mistakes and focus on fixing them rather than making excuses

Win Together

  • We value input from everyone involved in hiring - HR brings process expertise, departments bring technical knowledge
  • We work closely with Finance, managers, and leadership to solve organisational challenges
  • We celebrate the whole team's success when we hire great people or roll out improvements
  • We approach every interaction with respect and professionalism, even when conversations are difficult
