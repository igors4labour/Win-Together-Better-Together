About Product

Here's a quick rundown of what the Product Team does and how we fit into everything else going on around here. It tells you what we do, who we work closely with, and how we help shape the direction of our products.

Our Team Values in Action

‚ñº

Ignite Growth

  • We continuously learn from user feedback and use those insights to improve our product roadmap
  • We share what works (and what doesn't) across feature releases so everyone benefits from our learnings
  • We challenge ourselves to find better solutions for users, not just build what's been requested

Empower Through Innovation

  • We take time to understand what users really need before defining features and requirements
  • We look for practical product solutions that balance user needs with technical feasibility
  • When a product direction isn't delivering value, we're willing to pivot and try different approaches

Always Go Beyond

  • We put ourselves in users' shoes when making product decisions and prioritisation choices
  • We respond quickly to critical user issues and feature requests, knowing that delays impact their work
  • We focus our energy on features that genuinely improve user experience and business value

Trust Starts With Us

  • When we see potential product issues (usability problems, feature gaps, technical limitations), we flag them early rather than waiting
  • We keep stakeholders informed about roadmap progress and changes, even when timelines slip
  • When we commit to delivery timelines, we do everything we can to follow through
  • We own our product mistakes and prioritisation errors and focus on fixing them rather than making excuses

Win Together

  • We value input from Engineering, users, and all teams - Product brings strategy expertise, others bring execution and market knowledge
  • We work closely with Engineering, Sales, and Customer Success to deliver features that meet both user and business needs
  • We celebrate the whole team's success when we ship great features that users love
  • We approach every product discussion with respect and professionalism, even when making difficult prioritisation decisions
