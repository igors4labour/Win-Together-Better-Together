About Infrastructure

Here's a quick rundown of what the Infrastructure Team does and how we fit into everything else going on around here. It tells you what we do, who we work closely with, and how we help keep things running smoothly.

And if you're curious about anything else, feel free to pop over and ask.

Our Team Values in Action

‚ñº

Ignite Growth

  • Ensuring we have enough capacity for organic growth and plan ahead in accordance with the roadmap
  • We share what works (and what doesn't) in infrastructure management so everyone benefits from our learnings
  • We challenge ourselves to find better infrastructure solutions, not just maintain existing setups

Empower Through Innovation

  • We take time to understand what teams really need before implementing infrastructure changes
  • We look for practical infrastructure approaches that balance reliability with cost efficiency
  • When an infrastructure approach isn't working, we're willing to change course and try different solutions

Always Go Beyond

  • We consider the impact on all teams when making infrastructure decisions
  • We respond quickly to infrastructure incidents, knowing that downtime affects everyone's work
  • We focus our energy on infrastructure improvements that genuinely enhance system reliability and performance

Trust Starts With Us

  • When we see potential infrastructure issues (capacity problems, security risks, performance degradation), we flag them early rather than waiting
  • We keep teams informed about infrastructure status and planned changes, even when news isn't great
  • When we commit to infrastructure timelines or maintenance windows, we do everything we can to follow through
  • We own our infrastructure mistakes and focus on fixing them rather than making excuses

Win Together

  • We value input from Engineering, Product, and all teams - Infrastructure brings platform expertise, others bring application knowledge
  • We work closely with all teams to deliver infrastructure that meets both technical and business requirements
  • We celebrate the whole company's success when we deliver stable, performant systems
  • We approach every infrastructure discussion with respect and professionalism, even when managing competing priorities
