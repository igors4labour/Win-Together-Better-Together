About Sales

Here's a quick rundown of what the Sales Team does and how we fit into everything else going on around here. It tells you what we do, who we work closely with, and how we help bring new clients on board.

Our Team Values in Action

‚ñº

Ignite Growth

  • We continuously learn from sales conversations and use those insights to improve our approach and messaging
  • We share what works (and what doesn't) across the team so everyone benefits from our learnings
  • We challenge ourselves to find better ways to demonstrate value, not just repeat the same pitch

Empower Through Innovation

  • We take time to understand what prospects really need before proposing solutions
  • We look for practical sales approaches that focus on solving customer problems, not just closing deals
  • When a sales strategy isn't working, we're willing to adapt and try different approaches

Always Go Beyond

  • We put ourselves in prospects' shoes when understanding their business challenges
  • We respond quickly to prospect enquiries and follow-ups, knowing that timing impacts buying decisions
  • We focus our energy on deals that genuinely benefit from our solution, not just any opportunity

Trust Starts With Us

  • When we see potential issues (unrealistic expectations, poor fit, timeline conflicts), we flag them early rather than waiting
  • We keep prospects and internal teams informed about deal progress, even when things aren't going to plan
  • When we commit to follow-ups or demos, we do everything we can to follow through
  • We own our mistakes in sales conversations and focus on making them right rather than making excuses

Win Together

  • We value input from Marketing, Product, and Professional Services - Sales brings market intelligence, others bring expertise
  • We work closely with all teams to ensure smooth handovers and realistic client expectations
  • We celebrate the whole team's success when clients are successfully onboarded
  • We approach every sales conversation with respect and professionalism, even when prospects are challenging
