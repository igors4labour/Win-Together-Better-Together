About Engineering

Here's a quick rundown of what the Engineering Team does and how we fit into everything else going on around here. It tells you what we do, who we work closely with, and how we help keep things running smoothly.

And if you're curious about anything else, feel free to pop over and ask.

Our Team Values in Action

‚ñº

Ignite Growth

  • We continuously learn from production issues and use those insights to improve our systems and processes
  • We share what works (and what doesn't) across both development teams so everyone benefits from our learnings
  • We challenge ourselves to find better technical solutions, not just maintain existing code

Empower Through Innovation

  • We take time to understand what Product and clients really need before building features
  • We look for practical technical approaches that balance quality with delivery speed
  • When a technical approach isn't working, we're willing to refactor and try different solutions

Always Go Beyond

  • We consider the impact on users and business when making technical decisions
  • We respond quickly to production incidents, knowing that system downtime affects client operations
  • We focus our energy on building features that genuinely improve user experience and business value

Trust Starts With Us

  • When we see potential technical issues (performance problems, security risks, technical debt), we flag them early rather than waiting
  • We keep stakeholders informed about development progress and blockers, even when things are behind schedule
  • When we commit to delivery timelines or fix dates, we do everything we can to follow through
  • We own our bugs and code issues and focus on fixing them rather than making excuses

Win Together

  • We value input from Product, Testing, and users - Engineering brings technical expertise, others bring business and usability knowledge
  • We work closely with all teams to deliver solutions that meet both technical and business requirements
  • We celebrate the whole team's success when we ship great features or resolve critical issues
  • We approach every technical discussion with respect and professionalism, even when debating solutions
