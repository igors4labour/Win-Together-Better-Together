About Payroll

Here's a quick rundown of what the Payroll Team does and how we fit into everything else going on around here. It tells you what we do, who we work closely with, and how we help keep things running smoothly.

And if you're curious about anything else, feel free to pop over and ask.

Our Team Values in Action

‚ñº

Ignite Growth

  • We continuously learn from payroll processing issues and use those insights to improve our procedures and accuracy
  • We share what works (and what doesn't) in payroll processing so everyone benefits from our learnings
  • We challenge ourselves to find better ways to process payroll efficiently, not just follow existing routines

Empower Through Innovation

  • We take time to understand what clients really need from their payroll service before implementing changes
  • We look for practical payroll solutions that balance accuracy with processing efficiency
  • When a payroll process isn't working, we're willing to change our approach and try different methods

Always Go Beyond

  • We put ourselves in clients' and their employees' shoes, knowing that payroll errors cause real hardship
  • We respond quickly to payroll queries and discrepancies, knowing that people rely on timely, accurate payments
  • We focus our energy on activities that genuinely improve payroll accuracy and processing reliability

Trust Starts With Us

  • When we see potential payroll issues (data quality problems, processing risks, compliance concerns), we flag them early rather than waiting
  • We keep clients informed about payroll status and any issues, even when the news isn't great
  • When we commit to payroll deadlines, we do everything we can to follow through
  • We own our payroll mistakes and focus on fixing them rather than making excuses

Win Together

  • We value input from clients and technical teams - Payroll brings processing expertise, others bring system and client knowledge
  • We work closely with Engineering, Infrastructure, and Professional Services to ensure reliable payroll processing
  • We celebrate the whole team's success when payroll runs smoothly and accurately
  • We approach every payroll discussion with respect and professionalism, even when managing urgent issues
