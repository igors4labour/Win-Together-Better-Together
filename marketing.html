About Marketing

Here's a quick rundown of what the Marketing Team does and how we fit into everything else going on around here. It tells you what we do, who we work closely with, and how we help keep things running smoothly.

Our Team Values in Action

‚ñº

Ignite Growth

  • We continuously learn from campaign performance and use those insights to improve our marketing effectiveness
  • We share what works (and what doesn't) across campaigns so everyone benefits from our learnings
  • We challenge ourselves to find better ways to reach our audience, not just repeat the same campaigns

Empower Through Innovation

  • We take time to understand what our target audience really needs before creating content or campaigns
  • We look for practical marketing approaches that balance creativity with measurable business results
  • When a marketing strategy isn't delivering results, we're willing to pivot and try different approaches

Always Go Beyond

  • We put ourselves in our prospects' shoes when creating messaging and campaigns
  • We respond quickly to campaign requests and content needs, knowing that timing impacts business opportunities
  • We focus our energy on marketing activities that genuinely drive lead generation and business growth

Trust Starts With Us

  • When we see potential issues (campaign underperformance, messaging problems, website issues), we flag them early rather than waiting
  • We keep teams informed about campaign performance and lead quality, even when results aren't meeting expectations
  • When we commit to campaign timelines or content deliverables, we do everything we can to follow through
  • We own our marketing mistakes and focus on fixing them rather than making excuses

Win Together

  • We value input from Sales, Product, and customers - Marketing brings creative expertise, others bring market intelligence
  • We work closely with Sales to ensure campaigns generate quality leads that convert
  • We celebrate the whole team's success when campaigns drive business growth
  • We approach every marketing discussion with respect and professionalism, even when managing competing priorities
